Frequently Asked Questions

The Avengers Initiative recruits individuals with extraordinary abilities or skills. Candidates are typically identified through S.H.I.E.L.D. operations or recommended by existing members. Required clearance level is classified. Contact Director Fury for more information.

S.H.I.E.L.D. operates on a tiered clearance system: Level 1 (Basic Access), Level 5 (Field Agent), Level 7 (Senior Agent), and Level 10 (Director). Omega clearance is reserved for Avengers Initiative members and grants access to all classified materials.

In case of Code Red emergency: 1) Evacuate civilians to designated safe zones, 2) Contact nearest S.H.I.E.L.D. facility, 3) Await Avengers response team deployment. For imminent world-ending threats, Protocol 7 may be initiated by Director authorization.

Report suspicious activity or potential threats through the S.H.I.E.L.D. hotline (1-800-SHIELD-1) or submit a classified report via the secure portal. All reports are analyzed by threat assessment specialists and escalated to the Avengers if necessary.
