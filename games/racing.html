Racing Game Demo

About This Game

Race through stunning 3D environments with realistic physics and dynamic weather. Compete for the best lap times on challenging tracks.

Controls

  • Arrow Keys / WASD: Steer and accelerate
  • Space: Brake / Handbrake
  • Shift: Boost (when available)
  • C: Change camera view
  • R: Reset car position
  • ESC: Pause menu

Features

  • Realistic car physics and handling
  • Multiple racing tracks
  • Dynamic weather conditions
  • Time trial and race modes
  • Multiple camera angles
